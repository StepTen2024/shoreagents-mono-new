## **STEP 3: MANAGEMENT SCHEDULES INTERVIEW**

**Management Portal:**
1. Gets interview request
2. Sees client's preferred time (in client's timezone)
3. Management is in **Manila (MNL time)**

# ‚è∞ **DAY 1: THE MAGIC HAPPENS**

---

## **SHIFT-BASED RECORDING = THE KEY LOGIC**

### **The Problem:**
If staff works past midnight, what day does it count as?

### **The Solution:**
**Everything is recorded against the SHIFT START DAY**

**Example:**
- Shift starts: Thursday 10:00 PM
- Staff works until: Friday 2:00 AM
- **ALL activity = Thursday shift**
- No confusion, no split days
- Simple

**This applies to:**
- Clock in/out times
- Breaks
- Tasks completed
- Analytics
- Everything

---

## **DAY 1: ELECTRON APP & CLOCK IN**

**Setup:**
1. Management already installed Electron on staff PC (before Day 1)
2. Staff logs into Electron app
3. Staff clicks **"Clock In"** when shift starts

**From here:**
- Clock in/out tracked
- Breaks tracked
- Activity tracked (mouse, keyboard, apps, URLs)
- **Transparent for BOTH client AND management**
